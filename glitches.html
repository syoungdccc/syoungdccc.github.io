Glitches

Super Mario World

Before we get into any glitches, a little bit of backstory:

Super Mario World, as far as I can remember, is the first game where I was super fascinated by the glitches I saw within it, and what they could do. And that's partially in thanks to this video, which was a demonstration of the "Credits Warp" glitch. The idea that you could warp to the games credits in the first few levels was amazing to me. I had seen glitches like colors and such getting messed up before, but this was something else entirely.

So in tribute to it, I will be covering two of the glitches that make the credits warp possible. First up...

Item Swap Glitch

Super Mario World introduced Yoshi to the series. Mario can ride Yoshi and use his tongue to pick-up certain things (such as coins, power-ups, keys, etc.) Now, what if you wanted to get an item that Yoshi can't normally grab onto Yoshi's tongue? That's where the item swap glitch comes in.

If you collect/destroy an item on Yoshi's tongue, it's possible for it to be replaced by whatever the next sprite to spawn is. This is the Item Swap Glitch

Mario grabs a coin off Yoshi's tongue, and it's then replaced by a Chargin' Chuck enemy

On its own this doesn't do much, but the Item Swap Glitch is essential to the next glitch...

Item Stock Glitch

In the game, there are a few sprites that are mistakenly considered "power-ups" by the game, despite not actually being power-ups. If you use the Item Swap Glitch to get one of these sprites onto Yoshi's tongue, you can get a variety of effects upon Yoshi eating them. One of these effects is putting glitched/unintended items into your Item Stock/Item Reserve Box (which as its name suggests, is a box that can hold a power-up/item that can then be used by pressing SELECT, or taking damage)

Yoshi eats a Chargin' Chuck using the Item Swap glitch. This puts a goal orb that appears as a glitched koopa troopa sprite into Mario's Item Stock. The goal orb can then be placed using SELECT and collected to instantly end the level
